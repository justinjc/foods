Pork Slivers

Ingredients

Servings: 6

Instructions

  1. Step 1

    Marinade pork. Mince chili, garlic, and ginger.

  2. Step 2

    Cook pork slivers and reserve.

  3. Step 3

    Cook chili until oil is red. Add garlic and ginger and cook for 5m. Add 1 tbsp shaoxing wine and white part of leeks and cook for another 5m.

  4. Step 4

    Add cooked pork and green part of leeks. Mix.

  5. Step 5

    Add vinegar, MSG, and pepper.
